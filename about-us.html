About Us

At P's Caffeine, we believe that great code starts with great coffee. We know that programmers need fuel to power their creative minds, which is why we're dedicated to providing the best coffee experience for coders in town. Our coffee is carefully selected and roasted to perfection, and our baristas are experienced in brewing the perfect cup every time. We understand that coders need a comfortable, inspiring environment to work in, which is why we've designed our cafe to be the perfect workspace. We've got plenty of outlets, comfortable seating, and blazing fast Wi-Fi to keep you connected and productive. We also offer a selection of healthy snacks and treats to keep you going throughout the day. Come visit us at P's Caffeine and see why we're the go-to coffee shop for programmers in the area.

Product - We aim to serve the best quality and most interesting varieties of coffees, teas, espresso drinks and accompaniments at all times to everyone.

Service - We strive to treat our customers respectfully as our guests; we intend to offer our guests service that is prompt, efficient, friendly, and relaxed.

Atmosphere - We will provide a pleasant, welcoming environment, and will maintain a clean and attractive coffee house at all times.

Work Environment - We want P's Caffeine to be a great place to work. We aim to create a positive, respectful workplace and to empower our staff to be themselves and to achieve their fullest potential.

Community - P's Caffeine is committed to furthering the spirit and well-being of our neighborhood and the overall Cambridge community.
