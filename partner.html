Partners

P's Caffeine prioritizes relationships with suppliers to ensure transparency, social responsibility, and environmental sustainability in the supply chain.

Counter Culture Coffee - is a specialty coffee roaster and wholesale coffee supplier based in Durham, North Carolina, USA. Founded in 1995, the company has grown to become one of the largest specialty coffee roasters in the US, with a network of partners and customers across the country. Counter Culture Coffee is known for its commitment to sourcing high-quality coffee beans directly from small-scale farmers and producers around the world, and for its rigorous standards for coffee roasting and preparation. They offer a wide variety of single-origin coffees, blends, and decaffeinated options, as well as a rotating selection of limited release coffees. The company also provides training and support to its wholesale partners to ensure that the coffee is prepared and served to the highest standards. Counter Culture Coffee is widely recognized as a leader in the specialty coffee industry, and has won numerous awards and recognition for its quality and sustainability initiatives.

Espresso Parts - is a supplier of espresso equipment, parts, and accessories for cafes, restaurants, and home baristas. Based in Olympia, Washington, USA, the company has been providing high-quality espresso products to the coffee industry since 2007. Espresso Parts offers a wide range of products, including espresso machines, grinders, tampers, portafilters, and other accessories. The company is known for its commitment to customer service and support, and for its dedication to helping its clients succeed in the competitive coffee industry. Espresso Parts also offers training and consulting services to help businesses and individuals improve their coffee-making skills and knowledge. The company has a strong online presence, making it easy for customers to shop for and order their products from anywhere in the world.

La Boulangerie de San Francisco - is a bakery and cafe that specializes in traditional French baked goods, such as croissants, baguettes, and macarons. Founded in San Francisco, USA in 1995, the company has expanded to several locations across the city and offers a range of pastries, breads, sandwiches, and other French-inspired foods. La Boulangerie de San Francisco is known for its commitment to using high-quality, locally sourced ingredients, and for its dedication to traditional baking methods. The bakery has received numerous awards and recognition for its delicious pastries and breads, and is a popular destination for locals and visitors alike.

CODO Design - is a branding and design agency that specializes in serving craft breweries and other small businesses in the food and beverage industry. Based in Indiana, USA, the company has worked with clients across the US and internationally. They offer a full range of branding and design services, including logo design, packaging design, website design, and marketing strategy. CODO Design has a strong focus on storytelling and creating unique brand identities that reflect the personality and values of their clients. The agency's team of designers and strategists have deep knowledge and experience in the craft beer industry, and are dedicated to helping their clients succeed and grow. CODO Design has received numerous awards for their work, and is widely recognized as a leader in branding and design for the craft beer industry.

The Event Company - is an event planning and production company that specializes in creating and executing custom event experiences. Based in the United States, the company has extensive experience in organizing a wide range of events, including corporate events, galas, weddings, and more. They offer a full range of event planning and production services, including event design, vendor management, logistics, and on-site coordination. The Event Company's team of experienced event professionals work closely with their clients to ensure that every event is tailored to their unique needs and goals, and to create memorable experiences for attendees. The company has received numerous awards and recognition for their work, and is known for their creativity, attention to detail, and commitment to excellence.

Intelligentsia Coffee - is a specialty coffee roasting company that was founded in Chicago, USA in 1995. They are known for their commitment to sourcing high-quality coffee beans directly from small-scale farmers and producers around the world, and for their rigorous standards for coffee roasting and preparation. Intelligentsia Coffee operates several coffee bars across the US and has a strong online presence, selling their coffee beans and merchandise through their website. The company is also dedicated to promoting sustainability and social responsibility in the coffee industry, and has several initiatives in place to support the farmers and communities they work with.

Fairtrade International - is a non-profit organization that aims to improve the lives of farmers and workers in developing countries by promoting fairer trade practices. They work with small-scale farmers and workers to help them gain better trading conditions, fair prices for their products, and access to wider markets. Fairtrade International sets standards for fair trade products, and products that meet these standards are certified as Fairtrade products, which means they have been produced and traded according to ethical and environmental standards. The organization also provides support to producers and promotes sustainable development in their communities.
